  • Add some sample posts

    Before inviting people to the stream, add some posts of the nature you expect to see. For a project team group this will likely be status updates, lessons learned, and links to useful resources. For a discussion group you will want to be mindful that your objective is not only to inform but to engage employees in conversation. Whenever possible, post open-ended questions around topics that you think will prompt people to respond.

  • Email your target audience

    Sending an email directly to your target audience inviting them to participate in the stream and to fill out their profiles is an effective way to get a group on board. Include in the email the specific uses for the stream.
